Basic idea: Pic2Poem is a digital haiku writer. It uses your image and composes a haiku.

How does it work? Pic2Poem first segments the image into words (using Convolutional Neural Networks, CNN) and then combines words into a poem (using Recursive Neural Networks (RNN) trained on a haiku database).

Authors: Jordan Leitner, David Li-Bland, Maria Kazachenko {% endblock %}
